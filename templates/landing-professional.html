FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lumvi an AI chatbot?

Not exactly. A chatbot answers from a script. Lumvi is an AI Employee — it reads your business knowledge, has real conversations, and takes action: tracking orders, qualifying leads, and booking appointments.

Is the "AI Operating System" available today?

Today, Lumvi is your AI Assistant — answering customers, qualifying leads, tracking Shopify/WooCommerce orders, and booking appointments, live now. Sales automation, operations, and analytics are on our roadmap, not available yet.

Is this truly white-label?

Yes. On Growth and Agency plans you can use your own branding. Your clients will never see Lumvi's name anywhere in the product.

Do you take a commission on my clients?

Zero. You pay us a flat monthly fee. Charge your clients whatever you want and keep 100% of the margin.

What's the difference between Growth and Agency?

Growth ($149) gives you 20 client chatbots with full white-label. Agency ($299) is truly unlimited — add as many clients as you want at $15/seat beyond 20.

How does the AI work?

Lumvi uses intelligent AI models to understand what customers are actually asking — not just match their words to a list. It reads intent and context, so you get real answers instead of "I didn't understand that."

Can I test it before committing?

Yes — you can build and preview your Business AI in the dashboard before going live. Pick a plan and you're set up in minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards via Flutterwave, plus bank transfers on request.
