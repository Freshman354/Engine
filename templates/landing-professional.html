Frequently Asked Questions

Is this truly white-label?

Yes. On the Agency plan, you can map your own domain (e.g., app.youragency.com). Your clients will never see our logo or name.

Do you take a commission?

Zero. You pay us a flat monthly fee. You can charge your clients whatever you want (monthly retainer, setup fee, etc.) and keep 100%.

How is the AI trained?

You simply upload PDF documents, text files, or paste a website URL. Our system scrapes and indexes the content to create a knowledge base instantly.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all plans so you can build a demo bot and see how it works before paying.

Can I use it for my own agency site?

Absolutely. Many agencies use it for their own lead generation first, then resell it to clients once they see the results.

Do you provide support?

Yes. We provide technical support to you (the agency). You provide first-line support to your clients.
