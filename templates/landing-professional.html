FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is this truly white-label?

Yes. On the Agency plan, you can map your own domain (e.g., app.youragency.com). Your clients will never see our logo or name.

Do you take a commission?

Zero. You pay us a flat monthly fee. You charge your clients whatever you want and keep 100% of the margin.

How is the AI trained?

Upload PDF documents, text files, or paste a website URL. Our system indexes the content to create a knowledge base instantly.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — 14 days free on all plans. Build a demo bot and see real results before your card is ever charged.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit card payments, so clients anywhere in the world can pay easily.

Do you provide support?

Yes. We support you (the agency owner) directly. You provide first-line support to your own clients under your brand.
