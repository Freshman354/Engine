FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this truly white-label?

Yes. On the Agency plan you can map your own domain. Your clients will never see Lumvi's name anywhere in the product.

Do you take a commission on my clients?

Zero. You pay us a flat monthly fee. Charge your clients whatever you want and keep 100% of the margin.

How does the AI work?

Lumvi uses Anthropic AI to understand visitor intent and match it to your FAQ knowledge base — not just keywords.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — 14 days free on all plans. Build a demo chatbot and see real results before your card is ever charged.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards via Flutterwave, plus bank transfers on request.

Can clients see their own leads and analytics?

Yes. You can create client-facing logins on the Pro plan and above so clients can view their leads and FAQs directly.
