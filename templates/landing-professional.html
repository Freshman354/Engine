FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lumvi an AI chatbot?

Not exactly. A chatbot answers from a script. Lumvi is an AI Employee — it reads your store knowledge, has real conversations, and takes action: tracking orders, recommending products, and handling returns.

Does this work with Shopify and WooCommerce?

Yes. Lumvi connects directly to both, so it can pull real order status, product availability, and shipping info into the conversation — not generic answers.

Do I need a developer to install it?

No. Connecting your store takes a few clicks, and adding Lumvi to your storefront is one script tag — the same way you'd add a tracking pixel.

What happens when a customer asks about an order?

Lumvi looks up the order directly in Shopify or WooCommerce and answers with live status — no support ticket, no "let me check on that."

What's the difference between the plans?

Every plan includes the full AI Employee. Higher plans add more stores, product recommendations, webhooks, and priority support — see the full comparison above.

How does the AI work?

Lumvi uses intelligent AI models to understand what shoppers are actually asking — not just match their words to a list. It reads intent and context, so you get real answers instead of "I didn't understand that."

Can I test it before committing?

Yes — you can build and preview your Store AI in the dashboard before going live. Pick a plan and you're set up in minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards via Flutterwave, plus bank transfers on request.
